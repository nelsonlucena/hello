01  As-Is Assessment Chubb COG 04

The Demand Is Structurally Inflated

Much of today's volume never needed to reach an agent

Root Cause Analysis

Contact demand is inflated by avoidable contacts — customers calling because self-service is inadequate, documentation is unclear, and proactive communication is absent.

Fixing the front door reduces volume before it ever enters the center — the single largest structural lever.

Competitive Context

Leading insurers — Aflac, MetLife, Progressive — are deploying AI-augmented agents, conversational IVR and intelligent routing at scale.

Chubb's hybrid BPO model must evolve or risk customer attrition, particularly in A&H where digital-native competitors are accelerating.

03  Transformation Roadmap Chubb COG 11

Key Workstreams & Deliverables by Phase

Each phase ships concrete, measurable deliverables

01 — Foundation

Stabilise, unify and instrument the operation.

  • Contact-driver taxonomy across A&H, IPL, SPL with avoidable-contact % identified
  • Integrated agent desktop — single customer view (policy + interaction history)
  • Redesigned IVR targeting +20% containment in billing & policy inquiry
  • Real-time KPI dashboard: AHT, FCR, CSAT, containment
  • BPO scorecard and governance cadence aligned to objectives
  • Data foundation: unified customer ID, consent, interaction data lake
02 — Intelligence

Deploy AI and automate at scale across LOBs.

  • Conversational IVR (NLU) for top-5 A&H intents — FNOL, benefits, claims, payments, lookup
  • AI chat assistant, 60%+ containment for billing & policy inquiry (IPL/SPL)
  • Real-time Agent Assist: live transcription, knowledge retrieval, next-best-action, guardrails
  • Intelligent routing: skills-, intent- and propensity-based
  • Outbound proactive notifications — deflect 8–12% inbound
  • AI quality assurance: 100% call scoring replaces manual sampling
03 — Optimisation

Scale, embed and generate sustained value.

  • Predictive WFM model reducing overstaffing 12–18%
  • Churn-propensity model triggering retention offers at renewal
  • Cross-LOB opportunity ID: A&H customers surfaced for IPL/SPL
  • BPO contract renegotiated and right-sized to automation achieved
  • LLM fine-tuned on Chubb policy, claims and regulatory content
  • Mobile self-serve portal — policy changes, FNOL, billing
