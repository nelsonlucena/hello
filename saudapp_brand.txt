- Body: Inter, weight 400–500

TARGET PERSONAS
1. Mãe de família — worries about sick kids, no health plan affordable for family
2. MEI / Autônomo — self-employed, no employer plan, can't afford to get sick
3. CLT sem plano — salaried worker whose small company doesn't offer benefits

AD VISUAL RULES
1. Background: #2d3142 (preferred dark) OR #f4f6fa (clean light)
2. Accent: #1f87fe (electric blue) or #7fe3f0 (teal) for highlights, CTAs, prices
3. Warm emotional ads: use #ff9b90 coral as secondary accent
4. Logo: Always include "SaudApp" wordmark in #8c80f8 on dark bg; #7265e3 on light bg
5. Product representation: Phone mockup showing app, or lifestyle photography of person
   on phone consulting a doctor — NOT physical packaging
6. Typography on ads: Sora for headline (bold), Inter for body
7. Competitor/problem: red or #ff9b90 for X marks; brand accent for ✓ marks
